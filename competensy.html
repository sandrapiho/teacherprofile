Self-care and Professionalism

Performance indicators


1. Maintaining health - takes care of one's mental, physical, social and emotional well-being.

2. Maintaining balance ¬¨ takes care of one's work and private life balance.

3. Shaping a career path - consciously shapes one's career path and learning as a teacher, based on reflection on one's professional activities.

4. Updating knowledge - keeps oneself up to date with innovations in the field and teaching and learning, scientific literature and research.

5. Applying a research approach to developing one's professionalism - analyzes and improves one's teaching skills by applying a research approach.


Knowledge

Reflection and reflection models, knowledge of self-care (mental, physical, social, emotional), research, including action research methodology, data literacy and the ability to find and evaluate research-based information, legislation regulating teaching work (rights and obligations), teacher ethics

Learner Support

Performance indicators


1. Getting to know learners - gets to know each learner and group of learners, including their specific characteristics.

2. Building relationships, shaping the environment and caring for learners - builds good relationships with learners, creates a safe and inclusive environment for learning and cares about the development of each learner.

3. Planning and teaching - plans teaching pedagogically competently, based on the curriculum. Participates in the development of the curriculum. Treats the learning process as a whole and adapts it, based on the age, abilities, needs and mental and physical well-being of learners. Implements health-promoting activities, including active and movement-integrated learning activities, stretching and movement breaks.

4. Selecting, adapting, compiling and sharing learning materials - selects, adapts and compiles appropriate learning materials for learners, taking into account the specific characteristics of learners.

5. Content management ¬¨ addresses content in a scientific and evidence-based manner and generates interest in the subject and supports deep learning.

6. Value development and adherence - develops and adheres to values ‚Äã‚Äãagreed upon together with learners.

7. Development of general competences - supports the development of learners' general competences.

8. Enables learners in a targeted manner and actively guides them to take responsibility for their own learning and development. Is aware of the impact of stereotypes, including in their own attitudes, and avoids reinforcing them in their work.

9. Feedback and assessment - provides learners with constructive feedback on the learning process and learning outcomes and assesses learners using appropriate and learner-inclusive methods.


Knowledge


Basics of psychological functioning, motivational theories, developmental mindset, socio-emotional skills, general competencies and opportunities for their development, assessment and feedback options to support learning, including data literacy, the nature and implementation of inclusive education, curricula, knowledge of the field of study according to the type of education, teaching methods and principles for their selection in supporting deep learning, the nature and implementation of integration, the application of technology in supporting learning, legislation regulating learning and teaching.
